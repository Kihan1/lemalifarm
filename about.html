Who are We?

We are Lemali Farms company, running a cattle rearing business that is involved in commercial breeding of cows, bulls and calves.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading cattle rearing business brands in Tanzania. We intend to be known nationally for our quality services and to become among the Top 5 private leading livestock distribution business all over the nation of Tanzania

Our Mission

To buid a highly profitable and thriving livestock farming and distribution project whose target is to grow big in order to compete favourably with the leading cattle rearing and livestock farms in the industry both in the country and also outside the country






Our Core Values

  • Strive for openness, trusting communication to our employees and customers

  • Organic food is our primary goal

  • Preserve our environment and climate changes

  • We are obsessed with nature

  • Human dignity is our priority

  • Mutual respect and Professionalism
